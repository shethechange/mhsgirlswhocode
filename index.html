The first coding club for females (binary and trans-inclusive) at Milpitas High School. Founded in August 2018, the Girls Who Code community provides a safe, supportive environment for young girls to grow both professionally and personally. It is a place for girls to gain leadership positions and develop critical analytical skills while reducing the gender gap in STEM.
